title: 'Soergel Bimodules'
publishedAt: '2025-04-10'
summary: 'Soergel bimodules'
---

The **Hecke algebra** $\calh$ of a Coexeter system $(W,S)$ is the $\bbz[v,v^{-1}]$-algebra with generators $h_s$ for $s \in S$ and relations
- $h_s^2 = (v^{-1} - v)h_s + 1$ (quadratic relation)
- $h_s h_r h_s \ldots = h_r h_s h_r \ldots$ (braid relation)
